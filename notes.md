# I2C-Bus
- Nucleo connected pins
	- SCL: D15 - PB8 - CN10 pin 3
	- SDA: D14 - PB9 - CN10 pin 5


## ADC - ADS51115IDGS
### I2C-address
- Address: 1001000

# TMP102xxDRL
### I2C-addresses
- Address1: 1001011
- Address2: 1001001

# LED
- LED: LD2 - D13 - PA5

# PWM Signals
Choose signals in CN10:
- PWM1_HIGH: PC8
- PWM1_LOW: PC6
- PWM0_HIGH: PC5
- PWM0_LOW: PB15/PD8

# Conveter ON/OFF Signals
- PBOOST_EN: PA12
- PSEPIC_EN: PA11
